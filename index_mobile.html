What’s new

We use apple cider vinegar for your digestive health benefit!
For centuries, people have turned to apple cider vinegar for digestive health, but you cannot taste any acidic vinegar because we put it in stealth mode making our special soda beverage. Enjoy the fresh and rich taste of fruit, the healthiness of vinegar, and the coolness of canonic acid.

Orbitz’s Apple Soda

Orbitz made with carefully selected green apples grown in Ontario, which produces the world most delicious apple in the cool temperature of Great Lakes.

Orbitz Cranberry Soda

Cranberries are healthy food that eliminates inflammation in the body with powerful antioxdants. It is made by minimizing the astringent taste of it and maximizing the unique aroma and taste.

Orbitz Pineapple Soda

Pineapple not only is so sweet but also contains carbohydrates, proteins, fats, fibre, vitamin B and C, folic acid, potassium, and other nutrients. It is even more refreshing by adding carbonic acid to the most loved fruit in the world.

Orbitz Strawberry Soda

Efficacy of Strawberries not only does it prevents colds, but it also prevents melanin pigment from accumulating on the skin. Anthocyanins contained in strawberries play a role in stabilizing the eyes.
